Mission & Vision

Mission
Create a unique atmosphere for Pipa Lounge guests based on high standards of service quality. Giving a new, bright, gastronomic experience and immersing in fragrant, relaxing evenings filled with smoky hookahs of the highest quality. Everything should serve the idea that a guest coming here pauses the dynamics of everyday life, plunges into the pipa lounge atmosphere and dissolves into it (even if he came for half an hour). Pipa Lounge is a portal to another reality, it is an endless harmony of tastes, aromas and communication that allows our guests to enjoy life in the moment and come back to us again and again.

Vision
Create a network of spaces in which our guests will feel comfortable and safe, enjoy the gastronomic experience and relax by slowing the time down around them and immersing themselves in the atmosphere. Pipa Lounge is a point of attraction for many people, a place, where it is always delicious and guests are always welcome. Pipa Lounge is a religion based on the harmony of taste, visual and self-love.

Goal & Values

Goal
We work so that every guest of our establishment can feel cared for, disconnect from their affairs/problems and relax. For us, the highest manifestation of loyalty is a satisfied guests who returns again and again.

Values
Our team consists of specialists from different fields, we appreciate such things as:

  • Communication skills
  • Initiatives
  • Professional skills
  • Willingness to help a colleague
  • Carrying
  • Constant self-development
  • The ability to take responsibility
